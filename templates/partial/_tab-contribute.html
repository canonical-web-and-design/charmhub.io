Community participation is welcome on this project, here is how you can get involved…

Github

We keep our codebase in GitHub in the following repository:

https://github.com/canonical/openstack-dashboard

Feel free to create a PR and our moderators will take care of the rest. Your PR will be reviewed by our development team and if the changes are appropriate they will get merged!

Discourse

We use Discourse as our chosen community forum, feel free to get involved with discussion or create new topics. Visit the following link below to get started:

https://discourse.canonical-openstack.com
